Geological Diversity: Mineral Resources: Sedimentary Basins: Mineral Found Maps
  • Andhra Pradesh features diverse geological formations, ranging from ancient Archean cratons to sedimentary basins.
  • The state is known for its rich mineral resources, including limestone, coal, bauxite, granite, and rare-earth minerals. Geological research is active in mineral exploration and resource assessment.
