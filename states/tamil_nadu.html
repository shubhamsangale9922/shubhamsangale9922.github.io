Tamil Nadu

Geological Diversity Mineral Resources Sedimentary Basins Mineral Found Maps
  • Tamil Nadu boasts a diverse geological history, from ancient Archean cratons to more recent volcanic activity.
  • Abundant mineral resources include limestone, granite, garnet, and magnesite, with active research in mineral exploration.
  • The Cauvery and Palar Basins are significant for hydrocarbon exploration, attracting researchers in petroleum geology.
